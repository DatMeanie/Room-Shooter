Title: Room Shooter
Gameplay: The player is dropped into a semi-randomized linear map and tries to get a good time. Player must kill all enemies in a room to proceed.
It is a first person shooter and was created in 5 days.
This is a school assignment.
Created by Albin Fyhr

Features:
- Main Menu
- Functional UI
- Fast paced action
- Fluid movement
- Timer
- Functional assault rifle
- Several enemies and obstacles
- Snakes have AI
- Semi-randomized maps helps replayability
- Simple artstyle
- Music
